About Us

The youth have so much to offer to the world - we believe in the power that they hold, and know that they will mold future generations to come.But Mental Disorders like Depression and Stress create a large amount of strain on younger minds. At ListenUp, we are here to give them the support they need in order help them feel better in every way. We decided that the best way to tackle any issue is to just listen to them and let them vent. All you need to do is listen up and see how you can make an impact!

Explore.

We publish new articles on a daily basis. These articles deal with a vast amount of topics such as depression, addiction, and others. We also write guides and tips to help our young readers understand what to do and how to deal with their problems.
